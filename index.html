Welcome to Sunrise Global School. As a parent, you seek more than just grades; you seek assurance. Assurance that your child will be safe, understood, and prepared for a future we cannot yet fully imagine.

With my background in Psychology and Education, I believe that a child’s emotional stability is the engine of their academic success. At Sunrise, we do not just teach subjects; we teach students.

We blend the rigour of Critical Thinking & Innovation with the warmth of a supportive family. Supported by our 24/7 Nursing Officer and expert mentors, your child is in the safest hands in Jodhpur.
