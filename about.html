About Sunrise Global School

A 10‑acre campus in Jodhpur delivering character-led, future-ready education.

A Promise from the Principal

"At Sunrise, we teach students — not just subjects. Safety, emotional health, and curious minds come first. Our approach blends rigorous academic foundations with psychological wellbeing to help every child flourish."

Our Mission

To cultivate character, curiosity and critical thinking so each child thrives in a rapidly changing world. We provide personalised attention, modern facilities, and a values-driven curriculum.

Our Vision

Ethical leaders who are globally competent, technologically adept, and deeply rooted in humility and compassion.

Core Values

  • Compassion & empathy
  • Curiosity & creativity
  • Integrity & responsibility
  • Resilience & wellbeing

Facilities & Safeguards

Our secure 10‑acre campus features modern dormitories, STEM & robotics labs, a 24/7 medical officer, GPS-enabled transport, and nutritious vegetarian meals prepared under strict hygiene standards.
